  1. Home

G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering and Management

An automatic management system for bookstores

A system designed to automate a bookstore's daily chores such as maintaing the records and bills.

Different data structures such as arrays, stacks, heaps and linked lists were used to design this project.

CRUD operations such as update, delete, get and post were used to keep a record of the enteries in the database of the bookstore.

The time complexity of this application is kept optimal to reduce time consumption.
